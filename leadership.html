Our Leadership

Founders' Statement

Empowered Women Rising Trust Association was established during the COVID-19 era as a response to the need for unity, resilience, and mutual support among women. Despite challenges and setbacks, the vision endured, evolving into a registered organization committed to empowering women and uplifting the girl child.

Our journey reflects perseverance, faith, and the unwavering belief that when women rise together, communities are transformed.
